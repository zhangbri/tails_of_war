Gameplay

Beginner

Objectives:

  • Spawn units, command them, and capture buildings!
  • Capture villages to generate coins!
  • Use coins to spawn different units!

    • Win Conditions:

  • Eliminate Opponent's Champion
  • Capture Opponent's Capital

    • Artifact of Power

    A game-changer, this element imbues units with effects like consistent critical hits, terrain-independent movement, or a significant 30% defense boost.

    Holy Fish Altar

    A Stonehenge-style game location enhances matches with strategic benefits like team damage boosts, increased unit movement, unique gold payouts, and a 10% defense bonus.
